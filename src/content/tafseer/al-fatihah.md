Surah Al-Fatihah is the first chapter of the Quran and is recited in every unit of the five daily prayers. It opens with praise of Allah as Lord of all creation, affirms His mercy and sovereignty, and ends with a supplication for guidance to the straight path.

The chapter is often described as a summary of the entire Quran: it establishes the relationship between the Creator and the creation, and teaches the believer how to ask for guidance sincerely.
