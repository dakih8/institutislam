---
title: "Surah Al-Ikhlas — Sincerity"
summary: "A short chapter dedicated entirely to describing the oneness of Allah, said to be equal in reward to a third of the Quran."
source: "Tafsir Ibn Kathir (summarized)"
tags: ["Quran", "Tawheed"]
order: 2
---

Surah Al-Ikhlas consists of four verses that describe Allah as absolutely singular, self-sufficient, and without equal. It directly addresses the concept of Tawheed (the oneness of Allah) and rejects any notion of partners, offspring, or resemblance to creation.
