---
title: "Smiling Is Charity"
summary: "A hadith describing how simple acts of kindness, even a smile, are counted as charity in Islam."
source: "Jami' at-Tirmidhi"
tags: ["Character", "Charity"]
order: 2
---

The Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) said: "Your smile for your brother is charity."

**Explanation:** Islam defines charity broadly, extending beyond financial giving to everyday acts of goodwill. A sincere smile, kind word, or small act of assistance all carry spiritual reward.

**Benefit:** This hadith encourages believers to treat good character and warmth toward others as a form of worship, accessible to everyone regardless of wealth.
