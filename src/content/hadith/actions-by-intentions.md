---
title: "Actions Are Judged by Intentions"
summary: "One of the foundational hadiths of Islam, establishing that the reward of any deed depends on the intention behind it."
source: "Sahih al-Bukhari & Sahih Muslim"
tags: ["Foundational", "Intentions"]
order: 1
---

The Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) said: "Actions are but by intentions, and every person will have only what they intended."

**Explanation:** This hadith teaches that the value of an action in the sight of Allah is measured first by the sincerity and purpose behind it, not merely by its outward form. Scholars place this hadith at the very beginning of major hadith collections because every other act of worship depends on it.

**Benefit:** A believer is encouraged to renew their intention before any act of worship or good deed, seeking closeness to Allah rather than praise from people.
