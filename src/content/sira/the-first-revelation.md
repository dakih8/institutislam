---

At the age of forty, while in retreat at the Cave of Hira, the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) received the first revelation through the angel Gabriel, beginning with the command "Iqra" (Read/Recite). This marked the beginning of his prophethood and the gradual revelation of the Quran over the next twenty-three years.
