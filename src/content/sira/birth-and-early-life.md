The Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) was born in Makkah in the Year of the Elephant. His father Abdullah passed away before his birth, and his mother Aminah passed away when he was six, leaving him to be raised first by his grandfather Abd al-Muttalib, and later by his uncle Abu Talib. He was known among his people as "Al-Amin" (the trustworthy) even before his prophethood.
