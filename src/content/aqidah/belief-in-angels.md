Belief in angels is one of the six pillars of faith in Islam. Angels are created from light, do not disobey Allah, and carry out tasks such as recording deeds, delivering revelation, and taking souls at the time of death. Gabriel (Jibril) is noted as the angel entrusted with conveying revelation to the prophets.
