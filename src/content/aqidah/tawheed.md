Tawheed is the foundation of the Islamic creed. It is commonly explained in three dimensions:

1. **Tawheed ar-Rububiyyah** — affirming Allah alone as the Creator, Sustainer, and Controller of the universe.
2. **Tawheed al-Uluhiyyah** — directing all acts of worship exclusively to Allah.
3. **Tawheed al-Asma was-Sifat** — affirming Allah's names and attributes as described in the Quran and authentic Sunnah, without distortion or resemblance to creation.

Understanding Tawheed correctly shapes a Muslim's entire worldview, from daily worship to moral conduct.
