---
title: "Honesty (Sidq)"
summary: "The Islamic emphasis on truthfulness in speech and action as a defining trait of sound character."
source: "Islamic Ethics Encyclopedia"
tags: ["Character"]
order: 1
---

Honesty is repeatedly emphasized in the Quran and Sunnah as a marker of true faith. The Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) linked truthfulness to righteousness and warned against habitual lying, describing it as leading away from goodness. Muslims are encouraged to be truthful even when it is difficult, trusting that honesty ultimately brings ease.
