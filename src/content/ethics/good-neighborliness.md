---
title: "Good Neighborliness"
summary: "The Islamic teachings on the rights of neighbors and the importance of kindness within one's community."
source: "Islamic Ethics Encyclopedia"
tags: ["Community"]
order: 2
---

Islam places strong emphasis on the rights of neighbors, regardless of their faith. The Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) mentioned neighbors so often that his companions thought they might be included as heirs. Good neighborliness includes sharing food, checking on their wellbeing, and refraining from causing them harm or annoyance.
