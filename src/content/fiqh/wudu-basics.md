Wudu is the ritual washing performed before the five daily prayers and other acts of worship. The obligatory steps include washing the face, arms to the elbows, wiping part of the head, and washing the feet to the ankles, performed in order and without significant delay between them.

Recommended (sunnah) additions include saying "Bismillah" before starting, washing the hands and mouth first, and repeating each washing action up to three times.
